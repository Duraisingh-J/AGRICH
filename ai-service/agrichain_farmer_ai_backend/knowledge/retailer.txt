Retailers should monitor demand trends before placing bulk orders.

High predicted demand requires early reordering to prevent stockout.

Perishable goods require fast turnover to reduce waste.

Slow-moving inventory increases storage cost and wastage.

Festival seasons significantly increase demand for certain crops.

Dynamic pricing improves profitability during high demand.

Tracking expiry dates helps reduce waste.

If predicted price from distributors increases, adjust retail price accordingly.

Retailers should avoid overstocking during low demand periods.
